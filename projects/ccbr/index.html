Project Overview

Leading electronics and low-level control development for an open-source quadruped robot at Columbia College Botrobotics (CCBR). The project encompasses multi-layer power distribution PCB design, motor driver integration, CAN bus communication, and embedded control software — all targeted for future college-level educational use.

Power Distribution Board

The custom power distribution board manages battery power delivery to all subsystems of the quadruped robot, including motor drivers, compute modules, and sensor arrays. The board features voltage regulation, overcurrent protection, and status monitoring.
